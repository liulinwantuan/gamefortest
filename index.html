About the Game

Highway Traffic is an arcade-style driving game where you weave through fast-moving cars on a busy highway. It's simple, fast-paced, and addictive—play as long as you can without crashing!

Features

  • No installation—just open and play
  • Responsive design: works well on mobile and desktop
  • Increasing speed and traffic intensity
  • Perfect for short gameplay sessions

How to Play

Use arrow keys or swipe to change lanes. Avoid hitting other vehicles and see how far you can go!
