About the Game

Monster Survivors is a fast-paced survival game where you must fend off relentless waves of monsters in a thrilling arena-style battle. No downloads or installs required – just jump in and start playing. Ideal for fans of rogue-like shooters and action-packed gameplay.

Features

  • Instant play – no sign-up needed
  • Unique power-ups and character builds
  • Optimized for both desktop and mobile
  • Immersive Unity 3D gameplay experience

How to Play

Use your keyboard and mouse or touch controls to move, attack, and dodge incoming waves of monsters. Survive as long as you can and earn high scores to challenge your friends!
